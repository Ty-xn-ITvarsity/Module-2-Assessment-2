Welcome to Pine City Mall

Pine City Mall – A people-powered and community-centred destination in the heart of Johannesburg. Affectionately known as The Pine, we are home to approximately 200 stores of retail heaven. Discover the latest styles, beauty trends, delectable tastes, and stunning décor – all under one roof. With everything and more, Pine City is the ultimate one-stop, to-shop-it-all.
